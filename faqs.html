Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hx Fitness Studio?

Hx Fitness Studio is an online platform offering customized workout programs, diet plans, and personal training to help you reach your fitness goals.

How can I sign up for the programs?

You can sign up by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, filling in your details, and selecting the program that suits your goals.

Do I need any special equipment for the workout programs?

While most of our programs require minimal equipment, some advanced workouts may require access to basic gym equipment. Our trainers will guide you in the setup.

How do I get personalized nutrition advice?

By signing up for personalized training, you will also receive a tailored diet plan designed to complement your workout and help you achieve your fitness goals faster.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not satisfied with the program. Just reach out to our support team.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to our customer support team via email at support@hxfitness.com or visit the "Contact" section for other ways to get in touch.
