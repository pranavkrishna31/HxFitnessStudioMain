Diet & Supplement Guide

Macronutrient Portion Control

Proper portion control helps you stay on track with your goal — whether it’s fat loss, lean gain, or maintenance:

  • Protein: 1.6–2.2g/kg of body weight. Palm-sized portion per meal.
  • Carbs: Adjust by activity. Use a cupped hand to measure each meal.
  • Fats: 0.8–1g/kg. Thumb-sized serving of healthy fats per meal.
Macro-Dense Food Options

Choose nutrient-rich sources for each macronutrient:

  • Protein: Eggs, Chicken, Fish, Tofu, Paneer, Whey, Greek Yogurt, Lentils
  • Carbs: Rice, Oats, Potatoes, Fruits, Quinoa, Dalia, Whole Grains
  • Fats: Olive Oil, Avocado, Almonds, Walnuts, Seeds, Peanut Butter
Water Intake

Water is essential for digestion, recovery, and performance. Basic water intake guideline:

  • Drink at least 3–4 liters of water daily
  • Consume more in hot weather or during intense workouts
  • Monitor urine color — light yellow is ideal
Supplement Guide

Supplements can support your nutrition when used properly:

  • Whey Protein: To meet daily protein needs
  • Creatine Monohydrate: 3–5g daily for strength & muscle recovery
  • Omega-3 (Fish Oil): Anti-inflammatory, heart & joint health
  • Multivitamin: Covers basic micronutrient needs
  • Vitamin D3: 2000–5000 IU daily if sun exposure is low
  • Collagen: Supports skin, joints & connective tissues

NOTE - Also consider supplementation only if needed
